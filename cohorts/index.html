Water Boards Champions Cohorts

Champions Cohorts

The Water Boards Openscapes Champions Program is a mentorship and professional development opportunity for individuals and teams at the Water Boards.

Current and Upcoming Cohorts

2026 Fall Water Boards Cohorts: From mid-August through mid-October 2026, the Water Boards will host two Champions Cohorts.

  • Fall 2026 Tue AM Cohort
  • Fall 2026 Wed PM Cohort

Water Boards individuals and teams can apply to join these cohorts as mentors or participants starting May 1, 2026. Applications will close on Jun 30, 2026. To learn more:

  • Check out: Am I ready to be an Openscapes Mentor?
  • Check out: Is my team ready for Openscapes? which also applies to individuals who would like to apply to participate.
  • Attend one of the Roadshow Presentations scheduled for Jan - May 2026
  • Join an Openscapes Office Hour to ask questions and see if applying for a 2026 cohort is a good fit for you!
